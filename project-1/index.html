About Me


My name is Jesse Alexander Hernandez, I reside and grew up in Southern California. I have been designing for 10 years now, and what started as a hobby turned into my craft. Growing up, I was generally interested in all things geeky. Be it digital art, building websites, or video production. Using technology for creating fascinated me. Generally, I like seeing how things are made, and what makes them work. This lead me onto a road of books, online tutorials, and practice-practice-practice. I now design professionally, and I am happy to say that I truly enjoy what I do.
